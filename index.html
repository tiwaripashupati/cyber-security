Industry Report

Identified Business Problem

The business is facing a continuous and significant cyber security issue marked by frequent intrusions, malware detections, and Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks. The frequency and recurrence of these incidents indicate critical vulnerabilities in the company's current cyber security defenses. Additionally, the geographically dispersed origins of these attacks, targeting multiple protocols like ICMP, UDP, TCP, and DNS, suggest a coordinated series of threats. The inconsistent responses to these threats, with some high-risk incidents being ignored, further highlight the need for immediate evaluation and strengthening of the organization’s incident response system.
